Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online video game developed by Epic Games. It is available on a variety of platforms, including PC, Mac, Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo Switch. In Fortnite, players can choose to play solo, with a group of friends, or in a squad, and they can participate in various game modes, such as Battle Royale, Creative, and Save the World.
