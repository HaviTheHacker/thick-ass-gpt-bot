Fish

Fish are aquatic animals that are adapted for living in water. They can be found in oceans, rivers, lakes, and other bodies of water all over the world.

Types of Fish

There are many different types of fish, with a wide range of sizes, colors, and shapes. Some common types of fish include:

  • Sharks
  • Tuna
  • Salmon
  • Catfish
  • Goldfish

Interesting Facts About Fish

  • Fish are cold-blooded animals, which means that their body temperature is regulated by the temperature of the water around them.
  • Fish use their gills to extract oxygen from water. Some species of fish are also able to extract oxygen from the air using modified organs, such as a labyrinth organ.
  • Fish have a lateral line system, which allows them to sense vibrations and changes in water pressure. This helps them navigate and locate prey.
  • Fish reproduce in a variety of ways, including laying eggs, releasing eggs and sperm into the water, or carrying eggs in their bodies until they hatch.
